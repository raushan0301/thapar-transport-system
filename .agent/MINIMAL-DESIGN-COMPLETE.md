## 🎨 **Design Principles Applied:**

### **Color Scheme:**
- **Background:** White (`#FFFFFF`) and Light Gray (`bg-gray-50`)
- **Text:** Dark Gray (`text-gray-900`, `text-gray-700`, `text-gray-600`)
- **Primary:** Blue (`#1E40AF` / `bg-blue-600`)
- **Borders:** Light Gray (`border-gray-200`)

### **Typography:**
- **Headlines:** Bold, large, dark gray/black
- **Body Text:** Medium weight, gray
- **All text is easily readable**

### **Layout:**
- **Centered designs**
- **Clean white cards with subtle shadows**
- **Ample whitespace**
- **Simple, uncluttered**

### **Readability:**
- ✅ High contrast (dark text on light backgrounds)
- ✅ Large, clear fonts
- ✅ Proper spacing
- ✅ No busy backgrounds
- ✅ Logo is prominent and visible
